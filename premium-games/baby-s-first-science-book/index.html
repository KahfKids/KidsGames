Baby's First Science

A colorful journey through science for little explorers!

The Sun

The sun is very bright and warm. It gives us light during the day.

The Moon

The moon shines at night. Sometimes it looks round, and sometimes like a smile.

Stars

Stars twinkle in the night sky. They are very far away and very hot.

Water Cycle

Water falls as rain from clouds. The sun helps water rise back into the sky.

Plants

Plants grow from tiny seeds. They need water and sunlight to grow big and strong.

Butterflies

Butterflies start as tiny caterpillars. They change into a chrysalis, then become beautiful butterflies.

Animals

Animals live in different places. Birds fly in the sky, fish swim in water, and rabbits hop on land.

Weather

Weather changes every day. Sometimes it's sunny, rainy, snowy, or stormy.

Day and Night

Day is bright with the sun. Night is dark with the moon and stars. Earth spins to make day and night.

Magnets

Magnets can pull some things toward them. They have two sides called north and south.

Senses

We learn about the world with our five senses: eyes to see, ears to hear, nose to smell, tongue to taste, and skin to touch.

The End

You're a little scientist now! Keep exploring our amazing world!
